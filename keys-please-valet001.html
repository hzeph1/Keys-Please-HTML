Process

Four steps
to effortless
parking.

Our platform eliminates the friction of traditional valet — no paper tickets, no waiting, no ambiguity. Every step is tracked, verified, and secured.

01
Book Online

Reserve your spot in advance. Choose your venue, tier, and arrival time. Receive a QR token instantly via SMS.

02
Scan & Drop Off

Arrive and scan your QR code. Our attendant records a full walk-around video before the car moves — your legal protection.

03
Track & Request

Your Guest PWA shows real-time status. When ready to leave, tap "Retrieve" — we'll have your car at the door.

04
Pay & Go

Tip and pay seamlessly via Stripe. A full receipt with video link is sent to your email. Done.
